We are thrilled to announce that STEMpathy DNHS has been featured in LA Weekly for our outstanding achievement at the inaugural SoCal Youth Leadership Summit! Our team's dedication and innovative approach to STEM education earned us the prestigious Silver Award, recognizing our commitment to making a difference in the community.

## The Summit Experience

The SoCal Youth Leadership Summit, hosted by Ingenius Prep, brought together exceptional young leaders from across Southern California. Our team had the opportunity to showcase our initiatives and impact in STEM education, particularly focusing on our efforts to make STEM more accessible and engaging for all students.

## LA Weekly Recognition

The LA Weekly article highlights our achievement and the broader impact of youth leadership in education. As mentioned in the article, our success at the summit demonstrates the growing importance of student-led initiatives in shaping the future of education.

## Looking Forward

This recognition motivates us to continue our mission of promoting STEM education and creating opportunities for students to develop their leadership skills. We are grateful for the support of our school, mentors, and the broader community that has helped us achieve this milestone.
